What Gem Are You?
This effect is a classic "What/Who are you?" instagram filter. It's based on the popular Cartoon Network show "Steven Universe" (I'm a huge fan). The show is made up of a lot of gems and fusions- aliens that protect the earth (sometimes) and in the filter you get to figure out what gem/fusion you are.

You start recording and you are looped through a bunch of gems until the perfect one for you is found.

Built this using SparkAR Studio and a tiny bit of Javascript.
